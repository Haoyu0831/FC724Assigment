Purpose of Data Collection

The data collected on student progress at GIC is vital for assessing and improving the academic experience. By gathering feedback and analyzing performance, we aim to enhance learning outcomes and support students throughout their journey at GIC.

Benefits

  • Identify areas for improvement in courses and academic support services.
  • Enhance teaching methods and curriculum based on student feedback.
  • Provide personalized assistance to students based on their needs and challenges.
  • Contribute to the overall improvement of the student experience at GIC.
